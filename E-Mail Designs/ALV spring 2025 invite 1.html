Dear members,

Mark your calendars! On March 1st at 12:30, we will hold the General Assembly (ALV) of Trans Utrecht & Beyond. Unlike the event on the 8th of February, this event is only open to members.

During this meeting, we will make decisions on important topics that were discussed in the Public Meeting. Your vote matters, so make sure to be there!

The board is also elected at this meeting. There are currently no open board positions, but you can always register as a counter-candidate up to two weeks in advance.

The draft agenda will be sent out next week. There is still an opportunity to add topics. Do you have a proposal? You can submit it as a motion. Please send your motion no later than February 15th to voorzitter@transutrechtbeyond.nl. The motion format can be found here.

Save the date – we hope to see you on March 1st!
